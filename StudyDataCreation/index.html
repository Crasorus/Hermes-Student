Create Actuals

Generate sample data drop CSV files. Study Config and Supply Network must be filled in first.

CTMS Plan — ctms_plan.csv

Enrollment and visit schedule by site. Requires sites defined in Step 1.

ERP Inventory — erp_inventory.csv

Stock on hand, production orders, and shipments in transit. Requires items, depots, and lanes defined in Steps 1–2.

RTSM Actuals — rtsm_actuals.csv

Randomisation, dispensing, screen failures, and site inventory. Requires sites, arms, and items defined in Step 1.

Site Inventory — site_inventory.csv

Current on-hand snapshot and demand rates by site and item. Requires sites and items defined in Step 1.
