# RC-08 — Deviation Report Writer

## Purpose

Produce a structured deviation impact and remediation report following a deviation assessment.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent, GxP Compliance Manager (shared)

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- After future workflow — Deviation & Issue Response

---

## Inputs

1. **Deviation impact assessment** — From CO-06
2. **Relevant supply data** — Stock affected, patient impact

---

## Steps

1. **Lead with severity and scope** — How serious is the deviation and how wide is the impact?
2. **Describe the deviation** — What happened, where, when
3. **Present impact assessment** — Affected stock, sites, patients
4. **Describe remediation actions** — What has been done and what still needs to happen
5. **State follow-up requirements** — Monitoring, investigation, reporting obligations
6. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- Deviation report following the standard report template
- Audience: Quality, Regulatory, Supply Team

---

## Notes

- Deviation reports are formal documents — clarity and precision are essential
- Patient safety implications must be prominently stated
