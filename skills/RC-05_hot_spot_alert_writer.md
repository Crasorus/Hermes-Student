# RC-05 — Hot Spot Alert Writer

## Purpose

Produce a structured alert when hot spots are detected, with clear description of the risk and recommended actions.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent, Clinical Data & Insights Analyst (shared)

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- When AI-01 (Hot Spot Detector) identifies hot spots during WF-04

---

## Inputs

1. **Hot spot findings** — From AI-01
2. **Risk scores** — From AI-03
3. **Relevant supply data** — Stock positions, shipment status as needed for context

---

## Steps

1. **Lead with the most critical hot spot** — What is the most urgent risk?
2. **Describe each hot spot** — Location, pattern, severity, supporting data
3. **State the potential impact** — What could happen if no action is taken?
4. **Recommend actions** — Specific, actionable steps for each hot spot
5. **State urgency** — IMMEDIATE, THIS WEEK, THIS MONTH
6. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- Hot spot alert following the standard report template
- Audience: Supply Team, ClinOps, Management

---

## Notes

- Alerts should be concise and action-oriented — this is not a full report, it is a call to action
- Critical hot spots should be clearly distinguished from medium/low severity ones
