# RC-06 — Protocol Change Impact Report Writer

## Purpose

Produce a structured impact report following a protocol amendment assessment, documenting all supply chain implications and recommended actions.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent, GxP Compliance Manager (shared)

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- After WF-03 (Protocol Amendment Impact)

---

## Inputs

1. **Protocol change impact assessment** — From CO-05
2. **Demand re-forecast** — From DF-06 (if applicable)

---

## Steps

1. **Lead with the headline impact** — What is the most significant supply chain implication?
2. **Describe the amendment** — What changed in the protocol
3. **Present impacts by area** — Demand, inventory, logistics, compliance, labelling
4. **Quantify where possible** — How many additional kits, which countries affected, cost implications
5. **Recommend actions** — What needs to happen to accommodate the amendment
6. **State timeline** — When do changes need to be in place?
7. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- Protocol change impact report following the standard report template
- Audience: ClinOps, Regulatory, Supply Team

---

## Notes

- Protocol amendments can have urgent supply chain implications — the report should clearly flag time-sensitive actions
