# RC-03 — ClinOps Operational Report Writer

## Purpose

Produce the operational report for clinical operations stakeholders with detailed site-level and country-level supply chain information.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- Monthly or ad hoc (future workflow — Executive & Operational Reporting)

---

## Inputs

1. **All agent outputs** from the current cycle — demand, supply, logistics, compliance, insights

---

## Steps

1. **Lead with operational headline** — What does ClinOps need to know or act on?
2. **Present site-level detail** — Enrollment, consumption, stock levels by site
3. **Present country-level detail** — Aggregated view by country
4. **Highlight operational risks** — Sites at risk, shipment delays, stock-outs approaching
5. **Include enrollment vs. plan** — How is enrollment tracking?
6. **State actions required by ClinOps** — Site-level actions, escalations, decisions needed
7. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- ClinOps operational report following the standard report template
- Audience: Clinical Operations

---

## Notes

- This is the most detailed regular report — ClinOps needs site-level granularity
- Should complement, not duplicate, the executive report (RC-02)
