# RC-07 — Study Start-Up Report Writer

## Purpose

Produce the initial supply chain baseline report for a new study or new country, documenting the starting position and initial plan.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- During future workflow — Study Start-Up Onboarding

---

## Inputs

1. **Study configuration** — From DI-09
2. **Supply network** — From DI-06
3. **Initial demand forecast** — From the Demand Analyst
4. **Initial supply plan** — From the Supply Analyst

---

## Steps

1. **Present study overview** — Study ID, protocol, phase, countries, sites, treatment arms
2. **Describe the supply network** — Depots, manufacturing sites, key shipping lanes
3. **Present initial demand forecast** — Expected enrollment and kit demand
4. **Present initial supply plan** — Starting stock, planned orders, timeline
5. **Note key risks and assumptions** — What are the biggest unknowns at study start?
6. **State the monitoring plan** — Cadence of data drops and workflow runs
7. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- Study start-up report following the standard report template
- Audience: All stakeholders

---

## Notes

- This is the baseline document — all future reports will reference back to the starting position established here
