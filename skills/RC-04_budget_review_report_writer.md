# RC-04 — Budget Review Report Writer

## Purpose

Produce the quarterly budget review report that compares actual supply chain spend against the budget envelope.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- Quarterly (future workflow — Budget Review)

---

## Inputs

1. **Budget variance analysis** — From AI-07
2. **Waste estimate** — From SI-08
3. **KPIs** — From AI-05
4. **Supply plan** — Current and projected costs

---

## Steps

1. **Lead with budget status** — On track, over, or under budget?
2. **Present spend vs. budget** — Actual to date vs. envelope
3. **Break down by cost category** — Manufacturing, shipping, waste, other
4. **Forecast to completion** — What will total spend be at study end?
5. **Highlight cost risks** — What could push spend over budget?
6. **Recommend cost management actions** — If applicable
7. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- Budget review report following the standard report template
- Audience: Finance

---

## Notes

- Budget data may be limited — report what is available and flag what is not
