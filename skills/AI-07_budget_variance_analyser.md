# AI-07 — Budget Variance Analyser

## Purpose

Compare actual supply chain spend against the budget envelope and identify the top cost drivers and variance explanations.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Clinical Data & Insights Analyst

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- During future workflow — Budget Review
- When budget oversight is needed

---

## Inputs

1. **Budget envelope** — From DI-07 (policies)
2. **Actual spend data** — From available cost data in the study package
3. **Supply plan** — Current and historical orders and shipments

---

## Steps

1. **Calculate total actual spend** to date
2. **Compare to budget envelope** — What percentage of budget has been consumed?
3. **Identify top cost drivers** — What is the spend going on? (manufacturing, shipping, waste, etc.)
4. **Calculate variance** — Over or under budget, by how much?
5. **Forecast to completion** — At current run rate, what will total spend be at study end?
6. **Produce variance analysis** — Budget vs. actual with explanations

---

## Output

- Budget envelope vs. actual spend
- Variance (absolute and percentage)
- Top cost drivers
- Forecast to study completion
- Recommendations to manage cost (if over budget)

---

## Notes

- Budget data may not always be available — if missing, this skill reports UNABLE TO CALCULATE
- Feeds into RC-04 (Budget Review Report Writer)
