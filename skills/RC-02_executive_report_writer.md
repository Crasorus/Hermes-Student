# RC-02 — Executive Supply Chain Report Writer

## Purpose

Produce the monthly executive-level supply chain report that gives management and sponsors a high-level view of supply chain health, risks, and key decisions.

---

## Owner

**Primary:** Communications & Reporting Agent

---

## When This Skill Is Used

- Monthly or ad hoc (future workflow — Executive & Operational Reporting)

---

## Inputs

1. **KPIs** — From AI-05
2. **Trends** — From AI-04
3. **Risk scores** — From AI-03
4. **Supply plan score** — From SI-09
5. **Hot spots** — From AI-01 (if any)

---

## Steps

1. **Lead with overall supply chain health** — One sentence summary
2. **Present key KPIs** — Service level, waste, on-time delivery, cost per patient
3. **Highlight trends** — What is improving, what is deteriorating
4. **Summarise top risks** — Ranked by severity
5. **Note key decisions taken or pending** — What has the agent team recommended?
6. **State next steps and actions required**
7. **Include traceability**

---

## Output

- Executive supply chain report following the standard report template
- Audience: Management, Sponsor

---

## Notes

- Executive reports should be concise — detail belongs in operational reports (RC-03)
- Match the level of detail to the audience — executives need headlines and KPIs, not batch-level data
